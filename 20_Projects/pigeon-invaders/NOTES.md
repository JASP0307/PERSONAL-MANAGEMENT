# Pigeon Invaders — Notes

## Log

### 2026-07-19 — Prototype 1 results & v2 direction
- **Prototype 1**: 250mW red dot laser on a pan-tilt gimbal. Partially effective,
  but the target building is white — under sunlight the red dot washes out and
  is too weak to deter.
- **Lesson**: green wavelength is more visible and more recommended for pigeons.
- **v2 plan**:
  - Bought a 300mW green dot laser (slightly bigger than the red one) — waiting
    for it to arrive.
  - New 3D CAD design needed to fit the new laser.
  - Upgrading the pan-tilt gimbal to stronger MG996R servos.
  - Design a PCB for the main circuit to shrink the device and make it more robust.
